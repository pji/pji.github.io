Welcome to pjithoughts¶

A place for me to stow some thoughts. I make no promises that these are either good thoughts or amusingly bad thoughts. It’s even tricky to know how much they are my thoughts and how much they are taken from the thoughts of others. In other words, I don’t really know what this is. It just is.
